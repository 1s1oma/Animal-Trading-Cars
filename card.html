Honey Bee

Critical pollinators, honey bees pollinate 70 of the around 100 crop species that feed 90% of the world.

  • Scientific Name: Apis
  • Average Length: 12mm
  • Average Lifespan: 4-6 weeks
  • Habitat: Tropical climates, heavily Forested areas

Honeybees live in colonies with one queen running the whole hive. Worker honeybees are all females and are the only bees most people ever see flying around outside of the hive. Drone honeybees are all males. They live to mate with the queen. Honeybees are disappearing from hives due to colony collapse disorder
